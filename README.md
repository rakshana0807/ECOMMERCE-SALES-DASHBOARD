# 📊 Ecommerce Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive **Ecommerce Sales Dashboard** built using Power BI to analyze sales performance across multiple business dimensions such as time, region, product, and shipping.

The dashboard helps stakeholders monitor key metrics, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Track overall business performance using KPIs
- Analyze Year-to-Date (YTD) and Year-over-Year (YoY) growth
- Identify top and bottom performing products
- Understand regional and shipping-based sales distribution

---

## 📁 Dataset
The dataset includes:
- Order details (Sales, Profit, Quantity)
- Product information (Category, Sub-category)
- Customer segments (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office)
- Regional data (Central, East, South, West)
- Date table for time intelligence analysis

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Data Modeling (Star Schema)
- Data Visualization

---

## 📊 Key KPIs
- YTD Sales: $11.53M
- YTD Profit: $1.34M
- YTD Quantity: 107.2K
- YTD Profit Margin: 11.58%

---

## 📈 Features & Visuals
- KPI Cards with growth indicators
- Sales by Category (YoY comparison)
- Sales by Region (Map visualization)
- Top 5 & Bottom 5 Products
- Sales by Shipping Type
- Interactive slicers (Customer Segment)

---

## 🧮 Key DAX Measures
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin %
- YTD Sales
- Previous Year Sales (PYTD)
- YoY Growth %

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Sales slightly decreased (-0.83%), but profit increased (+4.50%)
- Profit margin improved, indicating better cost efficiency
- West region contributes the highest share of sales (~32%)
- Standard Class shipping dominates (~60%)
- Certain products consistently underperform and need review

---

## 🚀 Business Impact
This dashboard enables:
- Better decision-making through data insights
- Identification of high and low-performing areas
- Strategic planning for product and regional optimization
